# Madlibs
A game prompting for words from user.

```
Input template (or -r to randomize): Hello _name_! I hope you enjoy my _adjective_ game. Feel free to _action_ it. Have a _adjective_ time!

--- Prompts ---
name: Anh Tu Do
adjective: cold
action: play
adjective: wowful

--- Story ---
Hello Anh Tu Do! I hope you enjoy my cold game. Feel free to play it. Have a wowful time!
```
